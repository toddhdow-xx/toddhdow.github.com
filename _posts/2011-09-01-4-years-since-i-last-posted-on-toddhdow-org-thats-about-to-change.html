Folks,

I can't believe that this blog has been collecting dust for almost 4 years! Who knew that growing a new family from scratch would be so disruptive to one's priorities in life? So, for those that think I've been taking it easy for the last 4 years, I'm including a picture that shows you the project I call "family" that I've been cultivating for the last few years.

Regardless, I've been away for a while, and I'm getting antsy to write some new content. There are plenty of ideas percolating in my head. Well, here's a sneak preview of some topics that I'm working through right now:

  • I recently passed my PMP exam. I'll post my experience preparing for the exam;
  • I recently read an article in the Globe & Mail that discusses genetic testing and its impact on the value of a life and the pro-choice discussion. The article specifically mentions Cystic Fibrosis. CF is a disease that is very personal to me, so, I'll be wading into the bioethics, abortion and the pro-life / pro-choice discussion and offering my thoughts & views;
  • And, I'm writing longer stuff. I'm considering publishing something in the near future. So I'll be providing updates on that, along with some commentary on the publishing world. You can expect to read stuff pertaining to amazon kindle, kobo, goodreads and a bunch of other bookish topics;

So yeah... Feel free to peruse my existing site content. I'm surprised by how much traffic the existing content on this site still gets.

And, please do subscribe to my posts or check back often to keep up to date!
